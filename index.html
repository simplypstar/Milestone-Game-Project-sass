How To Play:
The object of Sudoku is to fill the 9-box squares, the 9-box rows, and the 9-box columns with numbers from 1 - 9. When entering the numbers, they cannot be duplicated in the boxes, rows, or columns. To begin, select a game to play from the Games dropdown at the top of the board. Click the 'Start The Game' button to load the board in its beginning state. Click on an empty space on the Suduko board. The background changes to yellow. Select a number button from below that corresponds with the number that you think should fill the space. The number on the button will be entered into the selected space. If you enter the correct number, the space will turn green for 2 seconds then return to its original color. If you enter an incorrect number, the space will turn red for 2 seconds then return to its original color. Continue filling the blank spaces for the remainder of the board. Spaces that already contain a number have been disabled and cannot be changed since these numbers are in the correct space. You can click the 'View Completed Game' button below to view how the board should look when completed.
